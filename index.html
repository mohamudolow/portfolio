About Me

Hello, my name is Mohamud Olow and I live in Vancouver, Canada. I am a front-end developer with 4+ years of experience in designing, maintaining and building websites and applications from scratch.



Technical Competencies

  • Modern JavaScript and it's core frameworks such as ReactJS, jQuery, D3.js and also ES6
  • Advanced CSS: CSS3, Boostrap Sass and Semantic-UI
  • Data Visualization & Concept mapping: D3.js, Vue Mindmap and CmapTools
  • Other Tools: HTML5, WordPress, Git, JSON and Axios
